DEFAULT HOTKEY: Ctrl+Alt+V
- Launches/toggles KlippBoard globally
- Works from anywhere in Ubuntu
- Configurable in app settings

CHANGE HOTKEY:
1. Launch: klippboard
2. Click ⚙ button in header
3. Enter hotkey (e.g., super+v, ctrl+alt+c)
4. Click OK - saved automatically

COMMON HOTKEYS:
- ctrl+alt+v (default)
- super+v (Windows/Super key)
- shift+alt+v (Shift combo)
- ctrl+alt+c (Ctrl+Alt+C)
